## Summary

The GX12-7 dual connector interface control document defines the mechanical connector standard, full electrical pinout, and three-bus software protocol that every compliant libdrone payload must implement. The ICD is covered across three articles: [[gx12-connector-standard]] (mechanical), [[payload-electrical-interface]] (pinout and limits), and [[payload-software-protocol]] (protocol contracts).
