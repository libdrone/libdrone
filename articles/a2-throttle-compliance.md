## Summary

This atom has been retired. The throttle-limiting mechanism it described now
lives in [[throttle-limiting]] as a general technical capability (beginner
training aid and speed ceiling). The regulatory question of whether and why a
speed limit applies to a given operation lives in the single source of truth,
[[legal-and-regulatory]]. There is no separate "A2 compliance" concept in the
corpus, because a privately-built libdrone does not operate in subcategory A2 —
see [[legal-and-regulatory]] for why.
